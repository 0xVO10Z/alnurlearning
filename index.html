Policies

Clear and fair guidelines

We aim to keep things simple, respectful, and mutually fair for all students and families.

❌ Cancellations

Please provide a 12 hour notice for cancellations. Cancellations with less than 12 hours' notice may be charged in full, except in cases of illness or emergency. We understand that things come up and will always try to be flexible where possible.

🔄 Rescheduling

Lessons can be rescheduled where possible. We'll always try to find an alternative slot that works for both parties.

💳 Payments

Payment is typically requested weekly or monthly in advance. Bank transfer and PayPal are accepted.

📋 Missed Lessons

Regular attendance is encouraged for the best progress. Consistently missed lessons may affect scheduling availability.

Full policy details will be shared upon enquiry or before lessons begin.
